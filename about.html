ABOUT US

Welcome to Car Market in Canada. Car Market treats the needs of each individual customer with great concern. Allow us to demonstrate our commitment to customer service! Our experienced sales staff is eager to share its knowledge and enthusiasm with you. We encourage you to browse our online inventory, schedule a test drive and investigate financing options. We offer a large variety of vehicles by Hyundai.


NO DEALERSHIPS

And no middle men

Cutting out the dealerships translates to lower cost for us on every vehicle we sell. What does that mean for you? We pass the savings on to you in four ways: lower prices, premium cars, a better experience, and no hidden fees.


CARS YOU CAN COUNT ON

Every car in our inventory is certified
